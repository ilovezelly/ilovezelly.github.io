Founding Story ☕️

I started my freelancing career way back August 2020. It was called DesignsbyDeozzell, been handling clients for almost a year. It was April 2021, when I started my course and it is also the time I rebrand my business. I enrolled in a class because I was having a hard time finding clients.

I did 6 months of freelancing as a full time social media strategist. But then I realized it was not my passion. And it is something that I am willing to do for the next 5 years of my life. I tried understanding myself even more, and as I looked back, I enjoyed the process of onboarding and making the system more organize. I want to help people have system in place because it would be a disaster if the don't have one.
